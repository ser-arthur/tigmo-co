Fuel Efficiency / Oil & Gas

NanoEfx: Revolutionizing Fuel Efficiency in Africa

As the exclusive Go-To-Market partner for NanoEfx in Africa, we are deploying patented catalytic fuel treatment to high-impact sectors. Our initial focus on Nigeria's Oil & Gas, telecoms, and government fleets addresses critical economic and environmental challenges by reducing fuel consumption, lowering emissions, and extending engine life.

TIGMO's Role:
  • Executing pilot programs with key industry players.
  • Securing local regulatory approvals and certifications.
  • Establishing local distribution and assembly infrastructure.

Energy Solutions / Manufacturing

ORLIB: Powering Africa's Energy Future

As the exclusive partner for Japan's ORLIB across Africa, we are empowering local manufacturing to meet a continental energy storage market projected to exceed $15 billion. We forge strategic alliances to integrate ORLIB's patented battery technologies, enhancing local product efficiency and competitiveness on a global scale.

TIGMO's Role:
  • Driving investment relations to fund local manufacturing.
  • Building strategic alliances with local producers.
  • Facilitating technology transfer and capacity building.
