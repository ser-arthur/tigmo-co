Oil & Gas / Technology

NanoEfx Partnership - Oil & Gas Market Entry

TIGMO Co. Ltd is the appointed Go-To-Market partner for NanoEfx in Africa, beginning with an anchored launch in Nigeria. Together were introducing NanoEfxs patented combustion-optimisation solutions to high-impact markets Oil & Gas fleets, telecoms backup generators, C&I power systems, and government transport/utility fleets.

This addresses a multi-billion dollar African Oil & Gas market, particularly strong in Nigeria, where large fleets and heavy engine use create significant demand for fuel savings and emissions reductions. NanoEfx's technology optimizes combustion to reduce fuel consumption, lower emissions (NOx/CO), and potentially extend engine life.

TIGMO's Role:
  • Run pilot programs in Nigeria for case studies.
  • Manage local regulatory approvals and standards.
  • Establish distribution and local assembly/packaging.
  • Deliver ROI measurement for enterprise customers.
