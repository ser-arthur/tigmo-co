Energy Solutions / Manufacturing

ORLIB: Powering Africa's Energy Future

As the exclusive partner for Japan's ORLIB across all 54 African nations, TIGMO is at the forefront of the continent's energy revolution.

The African energy storage market is on an explosive growth trajectory, projected to exceed $15 billion within the next decade. Our vision is not just to import, but to empower. We are forging strategic alliances with local African energy solution manufacturers to integrate ORLIB's innovative patented battery technologies, dramatically enhancing their product line efficiency, capacity, and competitiveness on a global scale.

TIGMO's Role:
  • Driving investment relations to fund local manufacturing.
  • Building strategic development alliances with local producers.
  • Developing the market to close the energy demand gap.
  • Facilitating technology transfer and capacity building.
